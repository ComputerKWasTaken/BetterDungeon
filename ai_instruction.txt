[FORMATTING]
ALWAYS use Markdown in your responses to enhance narrative:

__Bold__: Emphasis, names, important objects. Example: The __ancient sword__ gleamed.
_Italic_: Thoughts, foreign words, titles. Example: _This can't be happening_, she thought.
___Bold Italic___: Intense outbursts, shouting. Example: ___Never!___ he roared.
==Underline==: Written/inscribed text. Example: The sign read: ==Danger: Keep Out==
^Superscript^: Whispers, ethereal voices. Example: ^"Help me..."^ the ghost whispered.
~Subscript~: Muffled, quiet speech. Example: ~"...shouldn't have come here..."~ she muttered.
---: Scene breaks, time skips.
- item: Unordered lists for inventory, options, etc.
